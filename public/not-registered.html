What is Agentic Workflow?

Agentic Workflow is an intelligent automation layer for Jira that supercharges your team's productivity. Once your project admin registers your project, you'll get access to all these features:

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get notified via Slack DM, email, or SMS when issues are created, assigned, or updated — with priority-based routing.

🤖

AI Assignee Suggestions

AI analyzes your team's skills and workload to suggest the best person for each task.

💡

AI Solution Hints

Automated bug analysis and fix suggestions delivered right to the assignee's notification.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Generate performance reports, sprint summaries, and bug analysis dashboards.

📋 Tell Your Admin To:

  1. Log in to Agentic Workflow with their Jira admin account
  2. Select this project from the project list
  3. Configure feature preferences and create workflows
  4. Once set up, you'll be able to log in and provide your notification preferences
